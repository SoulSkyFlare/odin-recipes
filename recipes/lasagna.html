Lisa's Lasagna

Lasagna is an italian dish made of long sheets of pasta alternated with fillings, such as ragù, cheeses or vegetables. The dish is later assembled and subsequently baked in an oven, then cut into squares to be served.

Ingredients

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cans (6oz) tomato paste
  • 2¼ cups water
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 lasagne noodles, cooked and drained
